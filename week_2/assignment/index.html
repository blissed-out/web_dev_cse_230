About Me & Skills

Objective

Second-year BIT student focused on ML systems engineering: the compute, memory, and deployment constraints that determine whether a model actually works in the real world, not just in a notebook. Studying the physical, first-principles layer beneath modern AI (from TinyML/wearables through edge, mobile, and cloud). The world is rushing to build AI systems, not engineer them. AI is becoming like electricity, and electricity is useless without the grid to carry it. That grid — the infrastructure layer that makes intelligence deployable and accessible to the world — is what I am trying to help build.
